meet joy.

Joy Powell is a long time community activist,

pastor, mother, beloved friend and magnetic

social-justice trailblazer based in Rochester, NY.

Joy's call to action for justice stems from

loss of two children from murder, police

brutality, high rates of violence, injustice and

systemic oppression in Rochester, New York.

Through her organization, Equality and Justice For All,

Joy created a safe haven for

battered women, young children and families

living in poverty in upstate NY.

Joy has led numerous rallies, vigils,

community events and organized protests

from 1995 to 2006.
