Bedford Hills

june 14, 2025

Candle Light Vigil Showing Love and Solidarity for Our People Behind the Wall

Since October 2024 the Reverend Joy Powell Defense Committee

and HOLLA have held periodic vigils outside the prison,

each one attended by dozens of committed activists. In

order to focus attention on what is going on

at Bedford Hills we are hoping to generate a

participation that will number in the hundreds this time.

We believe that such an action could make a

tangible difference in the lives of the women and

trans community incarcerated in the prison. Please join us

in this effort.
